Platform Sahayak by AARVIX

The WhatsApp-First Automation Solution for India's MSMEs

The Problem

India's 6 crore MSMEs face two crushing pain points:

  • Leaky Bucket: Unpredictable cash flow due to scattered invoicing and late collections.
  • Data Entry Nightmare: Manual bookkeeping that consumes hours every week, is error-prone, and kills productivity.

The Solution

Instead of forcing business owners to learn another dashboard, Platform Sahayak works where they already work: inside WhatsApp.

Key Features:

  • ✓ WhatsApp-First Automation
  • ✓ Recover Cash (10-20% DSO reduction)
  • ✓ Save Time (eliminate manual data entry)
  • ✓ Outcome-Based Pricing
  • ✓ 1-Click Invoice Discounting (Pro tier)
